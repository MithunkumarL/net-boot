FINITE ELEMENT ANALYSIS

  • Remove unnecessary features (like fillets, holes, small details) to reduce complexity.

  • Assign accurate material data (Young’s modulus, Poisson’s ratio, yield strength, etc.).

  • Divide the model into finite elements, Ensure appropriate mesh density: finer mesh in high-stress regions, Perform mesh convergence study.

  • Analyze results: stress, strain, displacement, factor of safety, etc. Create contour plots, graphs, animations, Identify critical areas (max stress, failure points).

  • Compare FEA results with hand calculations or experimental data.
